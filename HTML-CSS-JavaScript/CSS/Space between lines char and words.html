Space b/w Lines/Characters/Words

  • You can increase or decrease spacing.According to your choice.

1.Space b/w lines

It specifies the space b/w the lines of a paragraph text.

  • By default the space b/w two lines is 1.
  • You can set it any value>1>value.
  • i.e. line-height: 0.8;

i.e. Intial paragraph before before spacing b/w Lines...

Today, I woke up at 8:30, drunked a glass of warm water, just after 5 minutes I went too the washroom.
After that I brushed my teeth, washed my mouth, taken some sushine.After that I talked with my friend,Then I started reading.

After spacing...

Today, I woke up at 8:30, drunked a glass of warm water, just after 5 minutes I went too the washroom.
After that I brushed my teeth, washed my mouth, taken some sushine.After that I talked with my friend,Then I started reading.
